Step 3:Time to build a site


WordPress is a free, plug-in based content management system, used to quickly build websites.

WordPress is popular because it meets the demands of many users with its flexible framework,which allows designers and developers to create and modify layouts and applications. Coupled with user-generated extension, websites are no longer limited by enterprise extension. There are more than 50,000 WordPress plugins (often free), such as slideshows, contain forms, SEO optimization,etc.
