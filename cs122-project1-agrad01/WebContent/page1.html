Step1:Picking a Domain Name


Your domain name is where customers reach you online

Choosing a domain name is similar to choosing a comapny name --- it requires a lot of thought and consideration. Your domain name is your identity on the web; you want to make sure you choose a domain name that not only fits ypur business, but is also easy to find and promote.

Instant Domain Search lets you check if domain are available.

Follow the steps below to help you pick the perfect domain name.

  • Make it easy to type
  • Keep it short
  • Use keywords
  • Target your area
  • Avoid numbers and hyphens
